Getting to the Venue by Tram

Catch any of the following trams at main station:

  • Tram 4 (to Gohlis-Landsberger Straße)
    Get off at the stop "Stallbaumstraße".
    Then take a short walk (300m) to Media Campus
    via Poetenweg.
  • Tram 12 (to Gohlis-Nord)
    Get off at the Stop "Fritz-Seger-Straße".
    It is approximately a five minute walk to Media Campus
    via Menckestraße and Schlösschenweg.

For further train timetable information and trip planning, visit Leipzig Public Transport websit here.
