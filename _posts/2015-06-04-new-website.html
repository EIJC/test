--- title: "Save the Date! November 6, 2015 | International Conference on Crisis Communication at EIJC" ---

<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="http://www.eijc.eu/conference-communication-floods/"><img class="alignleft wp-image-2625 size-medium" src="http://www.eijc.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/Logo-Fluttagung-duenn1-300x200.jpg" alt="Conference Communication Floods" width="300" height="200" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">EIJC is pleased to announce the date for the International Conference on Crisis Communication during the flood disasters 2002 and 2013 in Saxony, which will take place at the Mediacampus Leipzig on Friday, 6 November 2015.</p>
&nbsp;
<p style="text-align: justify;">The Conference is going to bring together actors and analysts of the flood disaster 2002 and 2013 in order to share experiences and analyses of the communication flows during this situation of crisis.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">We are very glad to welcome as speakers representatives of public authorities, the emergency management and volunteer organizations of Saxonia as well as international academic research contributions to the analysis of crisis communication.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The draft programme is <a href=" http://www.eijc.eu/conference-communication-floods" target="_blank">now online</a>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The official conference language will be German with English simultaneous interpretation.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">More information on the programme and how to register will be available <a href=" http://www.eijc.eu/conference-communication-floods" target="_blank">on the official conference website</a> in July 2015.</p>
